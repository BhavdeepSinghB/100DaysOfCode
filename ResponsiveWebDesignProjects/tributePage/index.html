A Summary of Dr. Zoidberg's life:

  • Born in a cluster of multiple zoids

  • Became a full grown crab/squid/lobster hybrid
  • Met Prof. Farnsworth in a war
  • Joined Prof. Farnsworth's team as doctor on board
  • Met Philip J Fry
  • Became a homeowner
  • Became really rich
  • Became really poor
  • Met the love of his life
